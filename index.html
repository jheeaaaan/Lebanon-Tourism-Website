About ———

A Nation of
Ancient Beauty
& Deep Roots

Lebanon is a small country in the Middle East located along the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is known for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, and cultural diversity. From ancient ruins and historic cities to mountains, beaches, and vibrant nightlife, Lebanon offers a unique mix of tradition and modern life.

Despite its modest size — roughly 10,400 km² — it holds within it one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities and a cultural heritage that stretches back over 7,000 years.

Today, Lebanon's soul lives in its people: warm, resilient, proud, and deeply connected to the land that gave the world the cedar tree and one of the most celebrated cuisines on Earth.
